About Me

For some reason in my mind the title 'Web Developer' almost carries as much weight as 'Rock Star' or even 'Super Hero'. Since I was never good at whaling on the electric guitar and never learned to fly, I decided at a young age that I wanted to do something creative when I grew up. So Here I am, a Rock Star ... Erm, I mean, Web Developer.

My Web Development Education is both formal and informal. I have a shiny nice piece of paper from college that says I know what i'm doing, but honestly, its the quality of work I do for my clients thats says the most. It's also the work I do for my clients that continues to teach me and help me hone my skills continually. I'm a big design junkie and would and would do this stuff for free if I could feed my family with my super powers ... But alas, I'm not a Super Hero.

Let me welcome you to wendellhernandez.com , my little space on the web. I really wanted to keep it clean and simple. Please take a quick look around, view the gallery, read more specifically about the services I offer, and then be sure to hire me.

I'm not kidding.
